Our Story

In This Corner
Since 1962

✦

Heritage Barber Co. has been cutting hair in this neighborhood since the spring of 1962. When George Calloway opened the doors on a Tuesday morning with two chairs, a fresh barbering license, and a pot of coffee on the shelf, he had one thing in mind: do good work, treat people right, and they'll be back. He was right.

Sixty-some years later, we're still here. Same corner, same belief. The chairs have been reupholstered three times and the prices have had to go up a little, but the fundamentals haven't changed: a clean cut, a close shave, and a straight answer if you ask us what looks good on you.

Three generations of men from this neighborhood have sat in these chairs. Fathers have brought in sons, those sons have brought in theirs. We know most of you by name. We aim to keep it that way.

"Walk in a stranger, walk out a regular."
